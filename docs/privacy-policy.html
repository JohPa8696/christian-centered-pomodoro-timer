Privacy Policy

Sacred Focus · Last updated: July 6, 2026

Sacred Focus ("the App") is a Pomodoro focus timer that shows Scripture during breaks. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains — plainly — what the App does and does not do with your information.

The short version

Sacred Focus does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. It has no accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no advertising. Everything happens entirely on your device.

Data we collect

None. The App does not gather any personal information and sends nothing to us or to any third party. There is no sign-up and no login.

Data stored on your device

To make the timer work and remember your preferences, the App saves a small amount of information locally on your device only:

  • Timer state (current phase, time remaining, session count)
  • Your settings (custom durations, chosen completion sound, chant preferences)
  • Focus history used for stats (counts and timestamps of completed sessions)

This information never leaves your device, is not accessible to us, and is permanently removed when you delete the App.

Notifications

With your permission, the App shows a local notification when a focus session or break ends. These notifications are generated on your device and do not involve any server or external service.

Third-party services

The App uses no analytics, no advertising networks, no crash reporting, and no social or tracking SDKs.

Children's privacy

The App collects no data from anyone, including children, and is suitable for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out via the project page: github.com/JohPa8696/christian-centered-pomodoro-timer.
